About Forte Haat

FORTE-HAAT is an e-Commerce and Market Research (e-CMR) Platform of CSVTU-FORTE, designed with the prime objective to promote rural and agro-enterprises of Chhattisgarh state with the concurrent objective of creating an ecosystem to train /promote/support rural and social startups in line with the objects of the CSVTU-FORTE. The Rural Entrepreneurship Development Centres (REDCs) are established in the affiliated institutions of CSVTU Bhilai to nurture and support students with entrepreneurial mindset with a vision of creating and developing an ecosystem focused on holistic rural empowerment through promotion of rural-based startups/ entrepreneurs. FORTE-HAAT will give an opportunity to associated students/alumni, staff, and faculty members of REDCs to get essential exposure, training and support in developing marketing and other essential entrepreneurial skills, in understanding the current market trends and provide detailed market survey reports on the specific products to the prospective entrepreneurs. CSVTU-FORTE invites interested Startups/ Self Help Groups (SHGs)/ FPOs /Entrepreneurs /Organizations to join hands as Channel-Partners in this noble initiative of promoting rural enterprises and indigenous Agro-based and Forest/Herbal products through FORTE-HAAT.
